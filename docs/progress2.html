Progress 2

For the second Progress report, you are to begin work towards Milestone 2, which involves designing and planning for your open source project.

During this week, you should focus on the first few tasks:

  1. Identifying related work.

  2. Enumerating any necessary resources.

  3. Determining your technology stack.

Given enough time, you should then focus on the remaining tasks:

  1. Outlining your architecture.

  2. Setting the goals for your MVP.

Deliverables¶

For this Progress Report, your group must upload their presentation slides to the group's project Google Drive folder. While you are not expected to have finalized all the details of your design choices, you should be able to describe some of your decisions and thoughts in regards to the topics above.
